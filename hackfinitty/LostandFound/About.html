Welcome to the Lost and Found Portal

Our project aims to simplify the process of reporting lost items and returning found ones. It provides a digital platform where users can quickly log items they've lost or found, view reported items, and get notified if a match is found. This helps reduce the stress of losing important items and increases the chances of recovery.

Whether it's a student ID, book, mobile phone, or keys, our platform ensures transparency, speed, and ease. It's designed with simplicity and usability in mind, making it easy for everyone to participate.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a smart, responsive, and reliable system to efficiently track lost and found items on campus. We aim to bring convenience, organization, and trust to students and faculty by using technology in a meaningful way.
